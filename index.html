SPIT

Get Ready!!

This game can ruin friendships, and you may lose a finger.

You may be thinking, "isn’t this a card game?”

Yes. Yes it is.

Objective

Get rid of all of your cards and land your opponent with the whole deck.

Play as many rounds as necessary to make this happen.

Set Up

One card at a time, create five stacks with increasing numbers. The left most stack should have one card, the right most stack should have five cards.

Flip over top card in each stack.

From here, like numbered cards can be moved to their match, as long as there are always five stacks. / ex. the 2 of Hearts and 2 of Clubs can be stacked, allowing you to flip over an additional card on your bare pile.

Your opponent will have the same setup.

Put excess cards between players in an individual draw pile to each player’s right. This will be used throughout the game
